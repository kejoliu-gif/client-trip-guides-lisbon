旅行概覽Trip Overview

關於里斯本About Lisbon

里斯本最精華的導覽圍繞兩個區域：聖喬治城堡（距您住宿很近）是里斯本的發源地，山頂要塞與阿爾法瑪老城區充滿古老歷史，常伴隨現場法朵音樂演出。辛特拉距此僅30分鐘，葡萄牙王室曾在此霧氣繚繞的森林山丘間建造夏宮，展示全歐洲最精美的浪漫主義建築。

Lisbon's best experiences center around two areas: Castelo de São Jorge (close to your hotel) is where Lisbon was born — the hilltop fortress and Alfama neighborhood are steeped in ancient history, often paired with live Fado music. Sintra, just 30 minutes away, is where Portuguese royalty built their summer palaces among misty forested hills, showcasing some of the finest Romantic architecture in Europe.
